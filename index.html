FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions
How much do you charge?
Generally speaking, a fully built Shopify store with standard features and app integrations will start at $4000.
Hourly work is priced at $75 per hour.
Monthly retainer work is priced the same as hourly.
Each project is unique so feel free to Get In Touch for a more accurate quote.
Can you do X, Y, and Z on my Shopify store?
I am able to do anything within the parameters of the Shopify and the Shopify Plus platform.
Check out the Services section for more details.
The short answer is yes.
Please Get In Touch for a quote.
How long will my project take to finish?
Each project will present its own challenges.
For a standard project, it should take about one to two months.
For larger and more complex projects, these will usually take three to six months or more.
Feel free to Get In Touch for a more accurate estimate.
Estimate or Fixed Price?
I tend to prefer fixed price quotes. The reason for this is that I would rather the cost stay the same for you whether or not any future issues might arise.
If the scope is very unclear or may change considerably, then I can give an estimate.
What payment methods do you accept?
I accept all forms of payment including bank/wire transfer, credit/debit cards, Paypal, and Venmo.
Why do I need an expert? Can’t Shopify Support help me?
Although Shopify Support is able to offer a very basic level of support, they will not be able to add custom features, apps, or code to your store.
If you would like a Shopify store that is tailored to grow your business, then I'd be happy to help.
Do I need a Shopify account to get started?
I work with all levels of business owners whether you have an established Shopify store, want to migrate your current business to Shopify, or are just getting started.
How will we communicate throughout the project?
I work online and remotely, not from a central office. Communication will initially be via email or phone. From there, we can switch to a different platform if you prefer, like Google Hangouts, Slack, Trello etc.
Do you offer ongoing support after the project is over?
Absolutely. I offer a retainer contract with a fixed number of hours per month. If you would like a quote for a retainer contract, feel free to Get In Touch.
How soon can we get started?
Send me message and I'll get back to you within 24 hours. I’m excited to hear from you.
